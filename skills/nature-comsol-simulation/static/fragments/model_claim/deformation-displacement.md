# Model Claim: Deformation and Displacement

Use when claims depend on displacement magnitude, stiffness, compliance,
deformation pattern, strain field, or shape change.

Check:

- Boundary conditions and load path are the main determinants of displacement.
- Clarify whether deformation is small-strain, large-deformation, linear, or
  nonlinear.
- Report extraction location, direction, norm, and reference configuration.
- Check mesh convergence and solver convergence for displacement and strain.
- For stiffness claims, require load-displacement relation or comparable metric.

Wording: "under the prescribed loading, displacement decreased..." is safer
than "the structure is stiffer in practice" without validation.
