# Simulation Domain: Fracture and Materials

Use for crack propagation, damage, cohesive-zone, phase-field, fatigue,
delamination, composite, microstructure, or material-failure simulations.

Check:

- Fracture/damage model, constitutive law, fracture energy, length-scale,
  cohesive parameters, fatigue law, or failure criterion.
- Mesh dependence of fracture path, process-zone resolution, crack-tip fields,
  and regularization length.
- Calibration data for material parameters and validation against experiments.
- Whether the result is path, load, geometry, or boundary-condition dependent.
- Reporting of failed convergence, snap-through, softening, or bifurcation.

Allowed strong claim: model-predicted crack/damage trend under calibrated
parameters and stated assumptions.

Risky claim: material toughness, lifetime, or failure mechanism without
calibration, validation, and mesh/process-zone evidence.
