# Simulation Domain: Structural Dynamics

Use for modal, harmonic, transient, vibration, resonance, damping, impact, or
fatigue-related mechanical simulations.

Check:

- Study type: eigenfrequency, frequency-domain, transient, response spectrum, or
  explicit/dynamic equivalent.
- Mass, stiffness, damping assumptions, constraints, and excitation definition.
- Time-step or frequency-resolution adequacy.
- Mode-shape interpretation, normalization, and whether modes are constrained by
  realistic boundary conditions.
- Validation against experimental modal analysis, impact test, accelerometer
  data, or literature benchmark when real resonance claims are made.

Allowed strong claim: predicted modal trends and relative frequency shifts under
specified assumptions.

Risky claim: operational vibration safety or fatigue life without representative
loading, damping calibration, and validation.
